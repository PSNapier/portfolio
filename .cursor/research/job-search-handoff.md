- Self-describes as **junior-to-mid level** in terms of market positioning
- Currently working a non-tech job at **$17.50/hour, ~30 hours/week** (~$525/week) — any remote tech role that meets or exceeds this income is a meaningful win

- **Backend:** Laravel (PHP), Livewire
- **Frontend:** Vue.js, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
- **Interests:** Also open to DevOps roles and data analysis roles, not just pure web dev

### Primary Targets
- Remote Laravel developer (junior, mid, or entry-level)
- Remote PHP developer (Laravel preferred but not required in the listing)

- Remote Livewire developer
- Remote full-stack developer (PHP/JS stack)

- Remote DevOps engineer (entry-level or junior)
- Remote data analyst (entry-level)

- Remote QA / testing engineer

---

## Search Strategy on Indeed

1. Go to **indeed.com**
2. Use search terms like:
   - `"Laravel" remote`
   - `"Vue.js" remote entry level`
   - `"PHP developer" remote junior`
   - `"full stack" Laravel remote`
   - `"DevOps" remote entry level`
   - `"data analyst" remote entry level`
3. Filter by: **Remote**, sort by **Date posted** (newest first)
4. Look for roles posted in the **last 7–14 days** to prioritize fresh listings

---

## Hard Filters — Skip These

- Any job that is primarily about **labeling data, training AI models, writing RLHF prompts**, or similar AI-training-as-a-gig work (e.g., "AI Trainer," "Prompt Engineer for training datasets," "Data Annotator")
- Roles requiring **in-person or hybrid** work
- Roles requiring **5+ years** of experience in a specific framework as a hard requirement

---

## What Makes a Good Match

A listing is worth surfacing if it has **most** of the following:
- Fully remote
- Uses PHP, Laravel, Vue, or Tailwind (or is entry-level enough that stack is learnable)
- No strict degree requirement
- Open to junior/entry-level OR doesn't specify seniority
- US-based or open to US remote workers (he is in the US, Texas timezone)

---

## How to Present Results

For each promising listing found, provide:
- **Job title**
- **Company name**
- **Direct link to the listing**
- **1–2 sentence summary** of why it's a good fit
- **Any red flags** to be aware of

Aim to surface **5–10 quality listings** rather than a long list of marginal ones. Quality over quantity.
