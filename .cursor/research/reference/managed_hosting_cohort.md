## Pattern summary (what market shows)

- Monthly subscription model for SMB websites commonly lands in the $99 to $199/month range.
- Most no-upfront offers use 12-month minimum commitments to recover build cost.
- Common included bundle: hosting + SSL + backups + maintenance + support + limited edits.
- Better margins come from tiering by:
  - included update hours
  - SEO/content growth services
  - priority support SLA
- Contract language often clarifies:
  - ownership/transfer terms
  - early cancellation/buyout
  - scope limits for unlimited edits

## Competitive notes for your positioning

- Your current $20 to $25/month managed hosting is far below mainstream SMB pricing.
- B2B-friendly baseline test:
  - hosting-only: $39 to $59/month
  - managed care: $99 to $149/month
  - growth/support tier: $199+/month
- Strong sales framing:
  - predictable monthly operating cost
  - no surprise emergency invoices
  - one partner for uptime, backups, security, and changes
