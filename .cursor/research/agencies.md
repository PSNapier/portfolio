## Execution notes (to improve interview conversion)

- Prioritize agencies with **real Laravel signal** over generic “full-stack” shops.
- For each target, prepare one tailored proof:
  - 1 project link
  - 1 short “problem -> implementation -> measurable result” blurb
  - 1 sentence on how you use AI/agents responsibly in delivery
- Keep a weekly cadence:
  - 5 high-quality applications
  - 3 warm outreach messages to agency leads/EMs
  - 2 follow-ups from prior weeks
